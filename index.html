Bulls and Cows

RULES: each player chooses a four digit number (i.e. 9230, 4572, 3821, 1058...) without 0 as a first digit (eg 0128, 0854...) and without repeating digits (eg 1192, 3555, 0266), after which in turns they will try to guess their opponents number. Each digit guessed in its correct place is a bull and a digit guessed correct, but in a wrong position is a cow. For example player2 number is 1092, player1 gives a guess: 1369? to which player2 will reply based on his number that player1 has guessed 1 Bull- 1369 and 1 Cow - 1369.
